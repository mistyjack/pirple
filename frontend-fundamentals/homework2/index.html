Pounded Yam Recipe

Pounded yam is a solid kind of food eaten in Nigeria. It's made from yam and molded into somewhat eliptical or spherical shape. I like it because of its texture and it's very sweet.

Ingredients

  • 600g of pounded yam flour
  • Four cups of water

Method of preparation

  1. Pour water into a pot and allow to boil
  2. Sprinkle the pounded yam flour into the boiling water and stir continuously untill the desired texture is achieved
  3. Sprinkle about a quarter cup of water on the dough, cover the pot, lower the heat applied to the pot and allow dough to simmer.
  4. Mix the dough thoroughly with a wooden spatula to achieve the desired smoothness
  5. Your pounded yam is ready!!!

Pounded yam is white in colour and round in shape. It smells like a perfectly cooked yam and tastes like a sugared sweet yam
